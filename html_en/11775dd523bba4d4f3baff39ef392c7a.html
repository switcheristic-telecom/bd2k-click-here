Overview:

Equal rights for homosexuals is one of the most controversial social topics in North America. Evangelical Christians consider it to be the most important social conflict at the present time -- more important to them even than restricting or eliminating abortion access for women. There is a massive division over what one polling company calls the "homosexual lifestyle." Barna Research found that 95% of Fundamentalist and other Evangelical Christians find the "lifestyle" to be unacceptable. This compares to 66% of born-again Christians and only 46% of the general population.
