The Apple Menu Items folder is sorted alphabetically, so if you've named your hard drive alias George, it may be hard to find in the middle of the list. To make it first in the list, add a space before the alias name. Or a better solution is to open a new text document (Stickies will do) and hit Return. Hit Select All (Command-A) and then Copy (Command-C); then paste (Command-V) the resulting invisible character in front of the alias name. Same effect, no ugly space in front.
